What we offer:

At Hot Beans Web, we provide a competitive salary and benefits package designed to attract and retain top talent. We are committed to your professional growth and offer numerous opportunities for career advancement and continuous learning. Our work environment is supportive and collaborative, fostering innovation and teamwork. You will have access to cutting-edge technologies and tools, and we offer flexible working arrangements, including remote work options, to help you maintain a healthy work-life balance. Additionally, we support further education and certifications to help you stay at the forefront of the web development field.

Job Specification

Front-End Engineer

As a Front-End Engineer, you will translate UI/UX design wireframes into actual code, producing visual elements of applications. You'll work closely with designers to ensure technical feasibility and optimize applications for speed and scalability. Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.js is essential.

Back-End Engineer

As a Back-End Engineer, you will design and implement robust and scalable back-end services and APIs. You'll work with databases, server logic, and third-party integrations. Experience with languages like PHP, Python, Ruby, or Java, and familiarity with databases like MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB, is crucial. You will ensure high performance and responsiveness to front-end requests.

Network Engineer

As a Network Engineer, you will maintain and administer our company's computer networks. Responsibilities include designing network configurations, troubleshooting issues, and ensuring network security and efficiency. Knowledge of network protocols and tools like Wireshark and Nagios is beneficial. You'll plan for future network growth to maintain robust and secure operations.

Search Engine Optimisation Engineer

As an SEO Engineer, you will enhance our clients' online presence by improving search engine rankings. Responsibilities include conducting keyword research, optimizing content, and using SEO tools to monitor performance. Strong understanding of SEO practices and tools like Google Analytics, Google Search Console, and SEMrush is essential. You will collaborate with content creators and developers to implement SEO best practices.
